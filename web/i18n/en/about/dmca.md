<section id="general">
<SectionHeading
    title="DMCA Policy"
    sectionId="general"
/>

SnapSave respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same.

SnapSave does not host, store, or cache any media content. we act as a proxy tool — no files are ever written to our servers. as a result, there is no hosted content for us to remove.

however, we take copyright concerns seriously and will act on legitimate requests where possible.
</section>

<section id="saving">
<SectionHeading
    title="Filing a DMCA Notice"
    sectionId="saving"
/>

if you believe SnapSave is being used to infringe on your copyright, please send a notice to:

📧 [support@snapssave.com](mailto:support@snapssave.com)

your notice should include:

1. **identification of the copyrighted work** you believe has been infringed.
2. **identification of the infringing material** — include the URL or link that was used.
3. **your contact information** — name, address, phone number, and email address.
4. **a statement** that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
5. **a statement**, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or are authorised to act on their behalf.
6. **your signature** (electronic is acceptable).

we will respond to valid DMCA notices within a reasonable timeframe.
</section>

<section id="responsibility">
<SectionHeading
    title="Counter-Notices"
    sectionId="responsibility"
/>

if you believe a DMCA notice was filed against you in error, you may submit a counter-notice to [support@snapssave.com](mailto:support@snapssave.com).

please note that submitting a false DMCA notice or counter-notice may expose you to legal liability.
</section>
