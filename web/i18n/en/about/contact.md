<section id="saving">
<SectionHeading
    title="What to Contact Us About"
    sectionId="saving"
/>

you can reach us for:

- **general support** — something isn't working as expected.
- **feature requests** — you have an idea that would make SnapSave better.
- **DMCA / copyright** — see our [DMCA policy](/about/dmca) for how to file a notice.
- **abuse reports** — if you believe SnapSave is being misused.
- **business enquiries** — partnerships, integrations, or anything else.

please be specific and include any relevant links or screenshots. vague messages are hard to act on.
</section>

<section id="responsibility">
<SectionHeading
    title="Bug Reports"
    sectionId="responsibility"

found a bug? you can also open an issue directly [on GitHub]({contacts.github}) — that's the fastest way to get a fix tracked and prioritised.

please include:

- what you were trying to download (platform + URL type, not the actual link).
- what happened vs. what you expected.
- your browser and operating system.

the more detail you provide, the faster we can help.
